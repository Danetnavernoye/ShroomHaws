Our Mushroom Journey

Founded in 2015, ShroomHaws began as a small family operation with a passion for mycology and sustainable farming. What started as a hobby in a backyard greenhouse has grown into a state-of-the-art facility cultivating over 20 varieties of gourmet and medicinal mushrooms.

We combine traditional growing methods with modern technology to produce the highest quality mushrooms while maintaining our commitment to environmental stewardship.
