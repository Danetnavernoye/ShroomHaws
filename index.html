Our Story

Passion for fungi since 2010

From Forest to Table

Founded in 2010, ShroomHaws began with a simple passion for the incredible diversity and benefits of mushrooms. What started as a small foraging operation has grown into a sustainable business that respects nature while bringing premium mushrooms to your table.

We practice ethical wildcrafting and sustainable cultivation methods to ensure the health of our forests and the quality of our products. Our team of mycologists and foragers are dedicated to bringing you the finest mushrooms nature has to offer.

At ShroomHaws, we believe in the power of mushrooms to nourish, heal, and delight. Join us on this fungal journey!
