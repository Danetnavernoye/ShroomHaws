Our Story

Founded in 2015, ShroomHaws began as a small family operation with a passion for sustainable agriculture and gourmet mushrooms. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a thriving business as demand for our premium mushrooms increased.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art facility that combines traditional growing techniques with modern technology to produce the highest quality mushrooms while maintaining our commitment to sustainability.

We're proud to supply restaurants, markets, and home cooks with fresh, flavorful mushrooms that elevate any dish.
